# Getting Started with VBMS Awards

This guide will help you get started with the VBMS Awards module.

## Prerequisites

### System Requirements
- **Web Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, or Edge (latest version)
- **Internet Connection**: Broadband connection recommended
- **Screen Resolution**: Minimum 1280x768
- **Operating System**: Windows 10/11, macOS, or Linux

### Required Access
- **PIV Card**: Personal Identity Verification card required for VA staff
- **Accreditation**: VSOs and representatives must have current VA accreditation
- **Training Completion**: Must complete required VBMS training modules
- **User Account**: Active VBMS account with appropriate role assignments

## Logging In

### Step 1: Access the System
1. Navigate to the VBMS portal URL (provided by your system administrator)
2. You will be redirected to the VA login page

### Step 2: Authentication
1. Insert your PIV card into the card reader
2. Select your certificate when prompted
3. Enter your PIV PIN
4. Click "Submit"

### Step 3: Multi-Factor Authentication (if required)
1. Enter the verification code sent to your registered device
2. Click "Verify"

### Step 4: Role Selection (if you have multiple roles)
1. Select the appropriate role for your session
2. Click "Continue"

## Basic Navigation

### Searching for Claims

1. Click "Search Claims" in the navigation menu
2. Enter search criteria:
   - Veteran Name
   - SSN or File Number
   - Claim Number
   - Date Range
3. Click "Search"
4. Results appear in a sortable table
5. Click on any claim to open it

### Opening a Claim

1. Locate the claim in your work queue or search results
2. Click the claim number or veteran's name
3. The claim details page opens with tabs:
   - **Summary**: Overview of claim
   - **Documents**: eFolder contents
   - **History**: Timeline of actions
   - **Awards**: Award information
   - **Notes**: Comments and annotations

### Working with eFolders

#### Viewing Documents
1. Click the "Documents" tab
2. Documents are listed chronologically
3. Click any document to view
4. Use the document viewer toolbar to:
   - Zoom in/out
   - Rotate pages
   - Download
   - Print
   - Annotate

#### Uploading Documents
1. Click "Upload" button in the Documents tab
2. Select document type from dropdown
3. Choose file(s) from your computer
4. Add description (optional but recommended)
5. Click "Upload"
6. Confirm successful upload

### Adding Notes

1. Click the "Notes" tab
2. Click "Add Note"
3. Enter your note text
4. Select note type (if applicable)
5. Choose visibility:
   - Private (only you)
   - Team (your team)
   - Public (all authorized users)
6. Click "Save"

## Processing Your First Award

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### 1. Select a Claim from Your Queue
- Open your work queue
- Look for claims with status "Ready for Award"
- Click to open the claim

#### 2. Review the Rating Decision
- Navigate to the Documents tab
- Locate the rating decision document
- Review for completeness and accuracy
- Check effective dates
- Note any special conditions or dependencies

#### 3. Verify Award Calculation
- Click the "Awards" tab
- Review the calculated award amount
- Verify calculation factors:
  - Disability rating percentages
  - Dependent information
  - Special monthly compensation
  - Effective date
- If incorrect, click "Recalculate" or "Edit"

#### 4. Review Generated Letter
- Award letter auto-generates based on rating
- Click "Preview Letter" to view
- Check for:
  - Correct veteran information
  - Accurate award amounts
  - Proper effective dates
  - Clear explanation of award
  - Appeals information included

#### 5. Quality Check
- Complete the quality checklist:
  - [ ] Rating decision reviewed
  - [ ] Award calculation verified
  - [ ] Letter content accurate
  - [ ] Veteran contact info current
  - [ ] All required documents present
  - [ ] Dependencies correctly recorded

#### 6. Approve and Send
- If everything is correct, click "Approve Award"
- Confirm the action
- System will:
  - Finalize the award
  - Send notification letter
  - Update claim status
  - Initiate payment processing

#### 7. Document Your Work
- Add a note documenting your review
- Include any special considerations
- Note if you made any adjustments

## Common Tasks

### Checking Claim Status
1. Search for the claim
2. View the "Status" field on the summary page
3. Check the "History" tab for detailed timeline

### Updating Veteran Contact Information
1. Open the claim
2. Click "Edit" next to contact information
3. Update address, phone, or email
4. Click "Save"
5. System may require verification

### Handling a Returned Claim
1. Claim appears in your queue with status "Returned"
2. Check the notes for reason for return
3. Make necessary corrections
4. Add note explaining changes
5. Resubmit for review

### Processing a Dependency Change
1. Veteran submits VA Form 21-686c
2. Claim appears in your queue
3. Review submitted evidence
4. Verify information
5. Update dependency information in VBMS
6. Award is automatically recalculated
7. New award letter generated
8. Approve and send

### Common Issues and Solutions

**Issue**: Cannot log in
- Solution: Verify PIV card is properly inserted, try different USB port, clear browser cache

**Issue**: Claim not appearing in search
- Solution: Verify search criteria, check spelling, ensure you have permission to access

**Issue**: Cannot upload document
- Solution: Check file size (max 25MB), ensure file type is supported (PDF, JPG, TIF), verify internet connection

**Issue**: Award calculation seems incorrect
- Solution: Verify all input data, check for updated dependency information, consult VETSNET data, contact supervisor if needed
